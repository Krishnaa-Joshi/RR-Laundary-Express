Healthcare

Healthcare Laundry Processing

Specialized hospital-grade laundry processing designed to meet strict infection control and hygiene compliance standards.

RFID Linen Management System

End-to-end RFID-based Linen Management System where every linen item is digitally tracked from issue to wash to return.

Automated tracking eliminates manual registers, paper-based errors, and billing disputes while delivering real-time inventory visibility.

Hospitals gain complete control, reduced shrinkage, and data-backed insights.
