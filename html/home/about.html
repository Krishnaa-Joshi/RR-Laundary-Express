Who We Are

About
RR Laundry Express

RR Laundry Express is a B2B laundry solutions specialist exclusively focused on healthcare and institutional laundry operations.

We combine RFID technology, automated workflows, and healthcare-grade infrastructure to solve the most critical challenges hospitals face in linen management.

We partner with hospitals to build smart, paperless, and fully traceable linen ecosystems that deliver measurable operational impact.

Our Mission

Reduce linen loss, eliminate paperwork, improve infection control, and optimize operational costs through automation.

  • Zero Linen Loss
  • Paperless Operations
  • Infection Control Compliance
  • Operational Cost Optimization
The Reality

Challenges in Hospital
Linen Management

High Linen Loss

Hospitals face continuous linen shrinkage with no clear explanation, leading to repeated procurement and rising costs.

Manual Registers & Paperwork

Paper-based tracking leads to human errors, missing entries, and operational inefficiencies.

No Real-Time Visibility

Hospitals lack real-time insight into linen movement across departments, laundry, and storage.

Billing Disputes

Inaccurate counting results in disputes between hospitals and laundry vendors.

Infection Control Risks

Poor traceability increases infection risks and non-compliance with hospital hygiene standards.

No Accountability

Without digital tracking, responsibility and ownership are unclear.

Our Core Solution

RFID-Based Linen
Management System (LMS)

A fully automated, RFID-powered platform that digitally tracks every linen item from issue to wash to return.

  • ✔ RFID tags embedded in every linen item
  • ✔ Automated tracking at every touchpoint
  • ✔ App-based system – zero paperwork
  • ✔ Real-time inventory visibility
  • ✔ Accurate billing & usage analytics
  • ✔ Complete linen lifecycle traceability & audit-ready reports

This system eliminates manual errors, reduces linen losses, and ensures complete transparency between hospitals and laundry operations.
