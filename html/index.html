How Our RFID Linen Management
System Works

A fully digital, RFID-enabled linen lifecycle management system designed to eliminate losses, improve hygiene compliance, and deliver complete operational transparency.

1

Linen Tagging & Onboarding

New linen is either pre-tagged by the manufacturer or tagged at the hospital using RFID technology. Each linen piece is digitally defined and added to the system using RFID tabletop readers, creating a unique identity for lifecycle tracking.

2

Issue & Movement Tracking

Linen issued to departments is automatically recorded using fixed or handheld RFID readers. Every movement ‚Äî issue, return, or transfer ‚Äî is synced in real time with the hospital‚Äôs linen database.

3

Soiled Linen Collection

Soiled linen received at the laundry facility is automatically scanned and logged into the system. Manual counting is completely eliminated, ensuring faster intake, accuracy, and zero reconciliation errors.

4

Washing & Processing

Hospital-grade washing, drying, and finishing are performed using validated processes. Each wash cycle is digitally tracked to ensure compliance with infection control protocols and quality benchmarks.

5

Quality Check

Every processed linen item undergoes inspection before dispatch. Quality checks ensure hygiene standards, fabric safety, and service consistency across every batch.

6

Delivery & Reporting

Clean linen is delivered back to the hospital along with complete digital records, audit logs, consumption analytics, and performance reports‚Äîensuring total accountability and visibility.
