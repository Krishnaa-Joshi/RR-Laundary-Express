About Laundry Express

RFID-based Linen Management is a system where each linen item is embedded with an RFID tag and digitally tracked throughout its lifecycle — from issue to washing to return — providing real-time visibility and complete accountability.

RFID enables automated tracking at every touchpoint, eliminating manual counting errors and unidentified losses. Hospitals typically achieve a 30–40% reduction in linen loss after implementation.
